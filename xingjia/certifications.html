Certification & features

Quality and safety certified standing desks, built and tested to recognised standards so you can work with confidence.

Our certifications

Our frames and electrical components are tested to international office-furniture and safety standards.

EN 527

European safety and durability standard for office desks. Tests stability, load capacity, wobble and long-term durability under daily use.

CE-LVD

Complies with the EU Low Voltage Directive. Motor, control box, power supply and wiring meet strict electrical and fire-protection requirements.

RoHS compliant

Electrical components are free from hazardous substances such as lead, mercury and cadmium, for safer electronics and responsible production.

Singapore Green Label

Meets the Singapore Green Label Scheme: low formaldehyde emissions, environmentally responsible materials and healthier indoor air quality.

BIFMA compliant

Tested to BIFMA standards for office furniture. Rigorous load, wobble and cycle testing so the frame stays stable after years of daily height adjustment.

Product features

Height range 72–122 cm

Full sit-stand range for most users. Smooth, quiet motor with memory presets on the keypad.

Dual & single motor

Dual motor: 125 kg capacity, 38 mm/s. Single motor: 70 kg capacity, 25 mm/s. Choose based on top size and load.

Custom tops

Real wood and plywood options in multiple lengths, breadths, thicknesses and edge profiles, built to order.

Anti-collision

Built-in collision detection stops downward movement if an obstacle is detected, protecting the desk and your space.

Stable frame

Engineered steel frame with tapered legs and wide feet, with minimal wobble at standing height, even with dual monitors.

10-year frame warranty

We stand behind our build quality with a 10-year frame warranty and dedicated after-sales support in Singapore.
