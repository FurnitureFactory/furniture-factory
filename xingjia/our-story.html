Our story

Discover the journey that shaped who we are today: a Singapore factory building standing desks without the showroom markup.

2021

The vision

Furniture Factory began with a simple realisation: in Singapore’s fast-paced world, health should never be compromised for productivity. Long hours at fixed desks take a toll. We set out to build height-adjustable desks that promote a healthier way of working, without sacrificing focus or paying showroom prices.

2022

The craftsmanship

Our tops are built from carefully selected timber and engineered materials. We work closely with our partners to ensure every slab reflects a commitment to quality and responsible sourcing. Each desk is made to order in our factory: real wood, real build, real value.

2022

The innovation

At the heart of our desks is a smooth height-adjustable mechanism. Switch between sitting and standing in seconds. Dual and single motor options, memory presets and stable frames designed for daily use in homes and offices across Singapore.

2023

The impact

What started as a factory experiment became desks in hundreds of homes and offices. Customers tell us about better energy, less back strain and the satisfaction of knowing exactly where their furniture comes from, and what they paid for.

We are not just selling desks. We are building a healthier, more honest way to furnish your workspace, factory direct, one desk at a time.
